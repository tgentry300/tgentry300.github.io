The Biography of Taylor Gentry

Early life

I was born in Anderson, Indiana on November 3rd in the year one thousand nine hundred ninety three. I was raised on the north side of Anderson and attended Highland High School and Alexandria Monroe High School.

After High School

After High School I worked as an EMT from the age of 18 until the age of 22. At this time I attended Paramedic School at Indianapolis EMS. I have worked in many areas of Indianapolis as a paramedic.

Current Events

Currently I am a student at Kenzie Academy. I chose to attend Kenzie Academy as I have always been interested development and coding.
